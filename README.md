# Glaze

Welcome to Glaze, your go-to online platform for finding the perfect room to rent. Whether you're a student, young professional, or someone seeking temporary accommodation, Glaze is here to simplify your search, and help you secure a comfortable living space that feels like home. With a wide range of options and convenient features, Glaze ensures a seamless and enjoyable reservation experience.

Key Features:

Extensive Room Selection: Explore a vast selection of available rooms for rent. Filter your search based on preferences such as budget, location, room type, and amenities to find the ideal match for your needs. Discover furnished or unfurnished rooms, shared or private bathrooms, and other specific requirements that suit your lifestyle.

Real-Time Availability and Instant Booking: Check real-time availability of rooms and secure your reservation instantly. With Glaze's user-friendly booking system, you can conveniently select your desired dates, review pricing details, and confirm your reservation in a matter of minutes. Say goodbye to lengthy booking processes and enjoy immediate confirmation.

Reservation Management: Keep track of your reservations with Glaze's intuitive management tools. Access your booking details and modify reservation dates (subject to availability). Stay organized and have a seamless travel experience from start to finish.
